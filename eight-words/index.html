How to play

🔤

8 Words

2+ players

One player sees eight unrelated words and gives the shortest clues they can; the others guess them one at a time. Then try the next set in fewer clue-words than before.

🎯 The goal

  • Guess all eight words. Score by the total number of clue-words used — lower is better.

📝 Setup

  1. One player gets a list of eight unrelated words. (A phone can generate a fresh set.)

🗣️ Play it out

  • Take the words one by one. The clue-giver describes each in as few words as possible — never using part of the word itself.
  • Add up every clue-word spent across all eight. Try to come in under 20, then beat your record.

💡 Tips

  • A single perfect word beats three okay ones — save your breath for the hard entries.
  • Unrelated words are the point: clue-givers can’t lean on a shared theme.
