# Historical figures

Pictures of historical figures used by the picture questions. Keep this list in
step with the files in this folder.

## attila.jpg

Attila depicted as a crowned monarch in the *Chronicon Pictum* (the Hungarian
Illuminated Chronicle), c. 1358 — the lead image of his Wikipedia article. No
authentic likeness of Attila survives, so this is a medieval artist's
imagining rather than a portrait from life.

- Author: **Márk Kálti** (attributed), illuminator of the Chronicon Pictum
- Licence: **Public domain** — the work was created c. 1358, long out of copyright
- Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Attila_(K%C3%A9pes_kr%C3%B3nika)_(cropped).jpg

The image is unmodified apart from being the cropped portrait used as the
article's lead image. As a public-domain work it needs no attribution; this
note is a courtesy and to keep the provenance with the file.
