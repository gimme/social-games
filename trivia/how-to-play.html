How to play

Trivia is a deck you drive, not a game with fixed rules. You host and keep score; the phone deals questions and lets you choose what comes next. Tap the answer strip to reveal it, tap a flag to show the table, and pick any mix of categories and difficulties as you go.

🎮 Games to run with it

Who Knows Most? (knockout)
First correct answer scores. Two points and you’re through the round; the last player without two drops out. Repeat until one champion remains.
Category streak
Select one category and rattle through it — most right in ten takes it.
Flags round
Select only 🚩 Flags and see who names the most countries.
Just for fun
No score — pass the phone round and enjoy the arguments.

ℹ️ Good to know

  • It opens with nothing selected — pick a category to deal the first question.
  • Difficulty runs from Basic to Impossible. Impossible is off by default; switch it on for a brutal round.
  • Skipping a question swaps it out without advancing the count at the top.
  • The phone never keeps score — that’s yours to run at the table.
  • No question repeats until you’ve been through everything selected.
  • Tap a flag to throw it full-screen, then turn the phone to show the table.
