How to play

🥁

12345678 Rhythm

1+ players

A rhythm-and-memory challenge you can do solo or in a circle. Keep a percussion groove going while you count to eight — then fall silent on the numbers one by one, from the front, down to just 8, and build all the way back up. Break the beat or blurt a silent number and it’s back to the top.

🎯 The goal

  • Count to 8 over a steady groove, going silent on one more number each pass — down to just 8 and back up to the full count — without ever breaking the rhythm.

📝 Setup

  1. Set a groove: clap your hands, snap your fingers, slap your thighs, stomp the ground — or any combination you like — on every beat, or (more commonly) every other beat.
  2. Pick a steady tempo everyone can hold. Solo works too — it’s just you against the beat.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Count out loud over the groove: 1-2-3-4-5-6-7-8.
  • Next pass, drop the front number — keep its beat, just don’t say it: 2-3-4-5-6-7-8. Then 3-4-5-6-7-8, and on down until 1–7 are silent and you only say 8.
  • Then climb back up the same way: 8, then 7-8, then 6-7-8, then 5-6-7-8 … all the way to the full 1-to-8 again.
  • Speak a silent number, miss a beat, or break the rhythm and you start over from the top.

💡 Tips

  • Keep your body on the beat through the silent stretch — mark the missing numbers with your claps or a nod, never your voice.
  • Crank the tempo each time you make it back to the top to ratchet up the difficulty.
