How to play

🥁

12345678 Rhythm

3+ players

A group rhythm-and-memory challenge. Count to eight and back together on the beat — but each pass you drop another number, holding it silently in your head.

🎯 The goal

  • Count up to 8 and back down as a group, keeping the rhythm, with more and more numbers gone silent.

📝 Setup

  1. Sit in a circle and find a steady, clappable beat together.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Count 1-2-3-4-5-6-7-8 and back down to 1, one number per person on the beat.
  • Each time around, remove one more number — you still ‘pass’ on its beat, just silently.
  • Miss a beat, speak a silent number, or break the rhythm, and the whole group starts over.

💡 Tips

  • Keep your body on the beat even through the silent numbers — count with a nod, not your voice.
