How to play

🐺

Werewords

4+ players

Twenty questions with a hidden twist: one of the guessers secretly knows the word too. Find the maker’s word in time — then work out who among you already knew it.

🎯 The goal

  • The group: guess the maker’s secret word with yes/no questions before time runs out.
  • The maker: if the group fails to guess in time, you win.
  • The secret knower: you were shown the word too — help the group get there, but don’t get fingered as the one who knew.

📝 Setup

  1. The maker writes the secret word on a slip and mixes it with enough blank slips for every other player.
  2. Each guesser draws a slip in secret. Exactly one sees the word; the rest are blank.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Set a timer. The group fires yes/no questions at the maker, who answers truthfully.
  • Guess the word in time and the group beats the maker. Run out of time and the maker wins.
  • Either way, finish by discussing and voting on who secretly knew the word.
  • The secret knower wins if they dodge the vote — so help just enough to look like an ordinary guesser.

💡 Tips

  • As the knower, ask a wrong question or two on purpose — flawless questions give you away.
  • Keep the timer visible so the maker can’t simply stall their way to a win.
