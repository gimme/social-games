How to play

🙆

Heads Up

2+ players

A word is stuck to your forehead where only everyone else can see it. Race to guess what you are from their hints — or flip it around and give the hints yourself.

🎯 The goal

  • Figure out the word on your own head, using clues from everyone who can see it.

📝 Setup

  1. Each player gets a word only they can’t see — held to the forehead, or dealt secretly and kept face-out.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Take turns asking yes/no questions, or have the group shout hints, until you land on your word.
  • First to guess theirs wins — or beat the clock as a group.

🎲 Variants

Reverse hints
One player holds up an unknown word facing out; everyone else gives hints to help them guess it.
One-shot clue
Everyone gives a single one-word clue at the same time. You get exactly one guess.
Just One style
Hint-givers each write one word; duplicates are removed before you see them.

💡 Tips

  • Good hints aim at the one feature that pins the word down, not its most obvious trait.
