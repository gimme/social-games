How to play

🏆

Top 5 Picks

2+ players

Pick a juicy theme, then two players reveal their top picks one at a time. Everyone who doesn’t know the theme inside-out gets to guess too — and the experts react.

🎯 The goal

  • Surface the best picks for a theme like “animals with weird sounds” or “most rewatchable movies.”

📝 Setup

  1. Choose a theme (a phone can suggest one) and pick two ‘experts’ who know it well.

🗣️ Play it out

  • The two experts name their top picks one at a time, alternating.
  • Anyone less sure can throw in a pick too — and the experts react: a hit, or a miss.

💡 Tips

  • The fun is in the reactions — pick themes where people have strong, surprising opinions.
