How to play

🎯

I Can Name…

2+ players

A bidding game of nerve and knowledge. Claim how many things you can name in a category — or call your rival’s bluff. Then you’ve got 45 seconds to actually do it.

🎯 The goal

  • Win the bid and deliver, or correctly call someone who bid more than they can back up.

📝 Setup

  1. Pick a category — “countries in Africa,” “Pixar movies.” (A phone can serve one up.)

🗣️ Play it out

  • Players take turns bidding higher — “I can name 7,” “I can name 9” — until someone says cap to call the bluff.
  • The last bidder gets 45 seconds to name that many. Make it and they win the round; fall short and the caller wins.

💡 Tips

  • Don’t bid your true maximum — bid the number you can still hit while talking under pressure.
