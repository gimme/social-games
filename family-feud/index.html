How to play

📊

Family Feud

4+, teams

Name the most popular answers to a survey question. Two teams, three rounds, and a frantic Fast Money finish. Pull the survey boards from the web, or just ask an AI to invent them.

🎯 The goal

  • Guess the top survey answers to prompts like “Name something you bring to the beach.” The more popular the answer, the more points.

📝 Setup

  1. Split into two teams.
  2. Prepare survey questions with ranked answers and point values — find real data online, or ask an AI to invent a plausible board.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Play three rounds head-to-head. One player from each team faces off to win control, then their team fills the board.
  • Three wrong guesses (three strikes) hands the board — and its points — to the other team.

🏁 Fast Money

  • Two players from the winning side answer five questions against the clock — about 20–25 seconds each.
  • Aim for 200 points combined. The second player can’t repeat the first’s answers, and may pass and come back.
  • Short on players? Run Fast Money one-on-one.

💡 Tips

  • Prep more questions than you think you’ll need, and keep the boards hidden on the phone so the host reveals answers one at a time.
