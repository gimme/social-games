🙅

Don’t Copy My Answer

3+ players

Trivia with a twist of nerve: the host has a secret answer in mind. Give the same answer as the host — or a wrong one — and you’re out. Be right, but be original.

🎯 The goal

  • Answer correctly and differently from the host’s secret answer. Match the host or miss the question and you’re eliminated.

📝 Setup

  1. Use open questions with many valid answers — “name a country in Europe,” “a movie with a sequel.”
  2. The host secretly notes one answer before anyone responds.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Everyone gives an answer. Reveal the host’s pick.
  • Anyone who matched it — or got it wrong — is knocked out. Last one standing wins.

💡 Tips

  • Steer away from the first answer that pops into your head; that’s the one the host probably wrote down too.
