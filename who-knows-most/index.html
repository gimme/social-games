How to play

🧠

Who Knows Most?

3+ players

Fast-buzz trivia as a knockout. First correct answer scores; reach two points and you’re safe to the next round. Whoever’s left empty-handed drops out.

🎯 The goal

  • Be quickest to the right answers. Two points sends you through; the last player still scoring is eliminated.

📝 Setup

  1. Pick a host with a list of trivia questions. (Generate a themed set on the phone.)

🗣️ Play it out

  • The host reads a question; the first correct answer takes the point.
  • You get one answer per question unless the host lets you try again.
  • Hit two points and you’re through. Keep going until one player still hasn’t — they drop out, and a new round begins.

💡 Tips

  • Mix easy and hard questions so a single fast guesser can’t run the table.
