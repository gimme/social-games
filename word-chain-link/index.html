How to play

🔗

Word Chain Link

1+ players

Eight words in a row, shown only by their first letters. Each word links to its neighbours, so cracking one helps you crack the next. A few free letters, and the clock is running.

🎯 The goal

  • Reveal all eight words before time runs out, working from first letters and the links between neighbours.

📝 Setup

  1. Lay out eight linked words but show only the first letter of each. (A phone can build the chain.)
  2. Grant five free letters to hand out as hints during the round.

🗣️ Play it out

  • Each word relates to the one before and after it — solve the easy ends and squeeze toward the middle.
  • Spend free letters when you’re stuck. Beat the timer, then try a faster run.

💡 Tips

  • Look for a pair of neighbours that only fit one way — locking one link often unlocks two more.
