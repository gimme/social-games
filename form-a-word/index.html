How to play

⚡

Form a Word

2 players

Two players each lock in a letter, reveal them together, and race to shout a word that starts with the first and ends with the second.

🎯 The goal

  • Be first to say a real word that begins with letter one and ends with letter two.

📝 Setup

  1. Each of two players secretly thinks of a letter. (Or let a phone flip two.)

🗣️ Play it out

  • Reveal both letters in quick succession.
  • First to call a valid word — first letter to last — scores the point.

🎲 Variant

  • Form a name: the answer must be a name, matching the first letters of a first and last name.

💡 Tips

  • Stuck on a hard combo? Long words are easier than short ones — there’s more room to bridge the two letters.
