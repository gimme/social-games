How to play

🤝

Mind Meld

2+ players

Two minds, one word. Say a word at the same time, then keep saying the word ‘between’ your last two — until you both blurt the same thing.

🎯 The goal

  • Land on the same word as your partner, in as few rounds as possible.

📝 Setup

  1. Two players face off (more can play in teams). Agree a count-in: “snap, snap, word.”

🗣️ Play it out

  • On the count, both say a word at the same time.
  • If they differ, both aim for a word that sits between the two you just said — then go again.
  • Keep converging until you match. Fewer rounds is a better score.

🎲 Variant

  • 4+ players: everyone says a word at once; you win the round when a clear majority match.

💡 Tips

  • Don’t chase your own idea — chase the midpoint. The trap is both players drifting toward different ‘averages’.
