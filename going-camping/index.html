How to play

🏕️

I’m Going Camping

3+ players

Also known as Green Glass Door. The host has a secret rule that decides what you’re allowed to bring. Listen to what passes and what doesn’t, and crack the pattern.

🎯 The goal

  • Work out the host’s hidden rule. You’ve got it once you can name two allowed items in a row.

📝 Setup

  1. The host secretly picks a rule. (A phone can deal one from a big pile.)

🗣️ Play it out

  • Going round, each player says “I’m going camping and I’m bringing…” and an item.
  • The host says whether it’s allowed by the secret rule.
  • Get two allowed items in a row to prove you’ve figured it out — but don’t say the rule aloud and spoil it for everyone.

🎲 Example rules

About the words
Four-letter words · double letters · starts with M · alphabetical order · starts with a letter from your own name.
About how you say it
Say “uh” first · say “okay” somewhere · say it standing up · with a hand gesture · while looking away.
About the things
Things that float · things you can collect · old inventions · things for the beach · useless things to bring camping.

🎲 Variant

  • Two-player: each of you has your own secret category; take turns naming things that fit the other person’s rule.

💡 Tips

  • When you think you’ve got it, test the edges — pick an item that only fits if your guessed rule is right.
