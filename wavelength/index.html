How to play

🎚️

Wavelength

2+ players

The host pictures a secret number from 0 to 10, then answers a topic in character for it. Everyone else guesses the number. How close can you read their mind?

🎯 The goal

  • Guess the host’s hidden 0–10 number using only how they rate a topic.

📝 Setup

  1. The host secretly writes a number from 0 to 10. (Let a phone pick it to keep things honest.)
  2. Someone names a topic or spectrum — e.g. “how scary are spiders”, where 0 = harmless and 10 = terrifying.

🗣️ Play it out

  • The host answers as if they are their secret number — describing it, never naming it.
  • Everyone guesses the number. Closest wins the round; reveal, then pass the host role on.

🎲 Variants

Spectrum cards
Frame each round between two extremes (cold ↔ hot, useless ↔ essential) and guess where the host’s pick sits.
Team dial
Two teams alternate: one sets the clue, the other guesses, then the third team can steal by going higher or lower.

💡 Tips

  • As host, calibrate to the whole scale — save your most extreme language for genuine 0s and 10s.
